## What is Cyberwave?

Cyberwave is an all-in-one platform that bridges the gap between **physical robots** and **digital intelligence**. It enables you to create digital twins of your robots, build and test in simulation, train AI models, and deploy autonomous behaviors; all from a unified platform accessible through your browser or SDK.

Whether you're developing a single robot arm, managing a fleet of mobile robots, or conducting cutting-edge robotics research, Cyberwave provides the tools and infrastructure you need to move faster and more confidently from concept to deployment.

## Platform Capabilities

### Digital Twins & Simulation

Create virtual replicas of your physical robots that mirror behavior in real-time. Test control algorithms, path planning, and complex behaviors in physics-based simulations before deploying to hardware.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pre-Built Robot Catalog" icon="book-bookmark">
    Access a library of ready-to-use robot models including arms, mobile robots, quadrupeds, and drones
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Custom Robot Import" icon="upload">
    Bring your own robots by uploading URDF files and 3D models
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Physics Simulation" icon="atom">
    Test in realistic physics environments powered by MuJoCo
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Real-Time Sync" icon="arrows-left-right">
    Bidirectional communication between physical robots and digital twins
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### AI & Machine Learning

Train AI models using data collected from your robots, deploy them as controller policies, and control robots using natural language commands.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Data Collection" icon="database">
    Record teleoperation sessions and create structured datasets automatically
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Model Training" icon="graduation-cap">
    Train Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models in the cloud
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Autonomous Control" icon="robot">
    Deploy trained models as controller policies for autonomous operation
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Natural Language Commands" icon="message">
    Control robots using plain English prompts powered by VLM models
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Workflows & Automation

Build automated sequences of robot operations with visual workflow orchestration. Create complex behaviors without writing procedural code.

    Orchestrate operations across multiple environments and robots

  <Card title="Condition & Branching" icon="code-branch">
    Add logic and decision-making to your automated workflows

</CardGroup>
