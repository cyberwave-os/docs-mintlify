## What is Spot?

**Spot** is a four-legged mobile robot from [Boston Dynamics](https://bostondynamics.com/products/spot/) built for autonomous data capture and industrial inspection. It weighs 33.8 kg, carries up to 14 kg of payload across two mounts plus an optional arm, travels at up to 1.6 m/s, and runs about 90 minutes per charge with an IP54 rating for dusty, wet sites. It navigates stairs and uneven terrain autonomously and is fully programmable through the [Spot SDK](https://dev.bostondynamics.com/), making it a standard platform for facility inspection, monitoring, and remote operations.

On Cyberwave, Spot connects through the same digital-twin workflow as any other quadruped: add it from the catalog, pair the hardware, and stream pose, joint states, video, and sensor data into the dashboard in real time — with **autonomous waypoint navigation** available out of the box. Control it from the dashboard or the [Python SDK](/tools/python-sdk), and mix it with cameras, drones, and arms in the same [workflow](/use-cyberwave/workflows). See the [robotic dogs overview](/get-started/robotic-dogs) for the full picture.
