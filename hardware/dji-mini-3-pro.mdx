## What is the DJI Mini 3 Pro?

The **DJI Mini 3 Pro** is a sub-249 g camera drone from [DJI](https://www.dji.com/mini-3-pro). It pairs a 1/1.3-inch 48 MP sensor and up to 4K/60fps video on a 3-axis mechanical gimbal with tri-directional (forward, backward, downward) obstacle sensing, and flies up to 34 minutes on the standard battery. Its sub-249 g weight keeps it under the registration thresholds of many regions, making it an accessible aircraft for inspection, mapping, and aerial automation.

On Cyberwave, the Mini 3 Pro connects through the **Cyberwave Edge for DJI** Android app on the phone tethered to your DJI RC — scan the pairing QR from the twin's **Live** tab and the dashboard lights up with the live video feed, gimbal attitude, and GPS/pose. From there you can fly discrete actions and waypoint missions from the dashboard or the [Python SDK](/tools/python-sdk). See the [drones overview](/get-started/drones) for the full pairing flow and safety model.
