## What is the Standard Camera?

The **Standard Camera** is Cyberwave's generic camera twin — a single digital twin that represents any off-the-shelf camera, whether it's a USB webcam, an IP/RTSP camera, a depth sensor, infrared, or an industrial GigE Vision sensor. It lets you build vision workflows once and run them against whatever camera you have on hand, without per-model configuration.

On Cyberwave, you add a Standard Camera from the catalog, pair the device, and the CLI auto-detects the camera (USB, V4L2, RTSP, GigE Vision, RealSense, …) and installs the right driver. The live RGB feed — plus depth, IR, or stereo where available — streams into the dashboard, ready for [recording](/use-cyberwave/environment-editor/replay), [teleoperation](/use-cyberwave/environment-editor/teleoperation), and [workflows](/use-cyberwave/workflows). See the [cameras overview](/get-started/cameras) for the full vision pipeline.
