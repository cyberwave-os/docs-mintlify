## What is a Camera Twin?

A **camera twin** is a digital representation of a physical camera within your Cyberwave environment. It streams live video feed from your hardware camera into the platform, enabling real-time monitoring, data recording, and AI-powered vision workflows.

Cyberwave supports a wide range of cameras:

- **USB webcams** (Logitech, generic UVC-compatible cameras)
- **Laptop built-in webcams**

Once connected, your camera feed is available across the platform, in the environment viewer, for dataset recording, and as input to ML models.
