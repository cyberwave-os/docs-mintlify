## What is the DJI Mini 4 Pro?

The **DJI Mini 4 Pro** is DJI's sub-249 g flagship Mini drone, succeeding the Mini 3 Pro with omnidirectional obstacle sensing and ActiveTrack 360° subject tracking. It records 4K HDR and 4K/100fps slow-motion from a 1/1.3-inch 48 MP sensor on a 3-axis mechanical gimbal, and flies up to 34 minutes on the standard battery — see the [official specs](https://www.dji.com/mini-4-pro/specs). Its gimbal's pan axis is mechanically limited to ±30°, so large heading changes are made by rotating the aircraft (see the [drones overview](/get-started/drones) for the control implications). Its light weight and obstacle avoidance make it a strong choice for autonomous inspection and mapping routes.

On Cyberwave, the Mini 4 Pro connects through the **Cyberwave Edge for DJI** Android app on the phone tethered to your DJI RC — scan the pairing QR from the twin's **Live** tab to stream video, gimbal attitude, and GPS/pose into the dashboard. Discrete actions (takeoff, land, return-to-home, gimbal) ship out of the box; continuous-stick locomotion is opt-in per twin via DJI Virtual Stick. Control it from the dashboard or the [Python SDK](/tools/python-sdk). See the [drones overview](/get-started/drones) for the full pairing flow and safety model.
