## What is the UR7e?

The **UR7e** is a 6-axis collaborative robot (cobot) from [Universal Robots](https://www.universal-robots.com/products/ur7e/), introduced in May 2025 as the upgraded successor to the UR5e — same 850 mm reach, with payload raised from 5 kg to 7.5 kg. As an e-Series cobot it has a built-in force/torque sensor, ±0.03 mm repeatability, an IP54 rating, and 20 configurable safety functions (rated PLd, Category 3 per EN ISO 13849-1), making it a workhorse for machine tending, palletizing, assembly, and inspection cells that operate safely alongside people.

On Cyberwave, the UR7e connects through the same digital-twin workflow as any other arm: add it from the catalog, pair the hardware, and control it from the dashboard or the [Python SDK](/tools/python-sdk). Because it speaks ROS 2, the UR7e is also a good reference for bridging an existing industrial stack into Cyberwave via the [MQTT bridge pattern](/hardware/other-hardware/custom-hardware). See the [robotic arms overview](/get-started/robotic-arms) for the full lifecycle from teleoperation to trained policies.
