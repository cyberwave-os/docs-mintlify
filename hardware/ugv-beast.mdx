## What is the UGV Beast?

The **UGV Beast** is an open-source, tracked off-road unmanned ground vehicle (UGV) from [Waveshare](https://www.waveshare.com/wiki/UGV-Beast). It uses a dual-controller design — a Raspberry Pi (4B/5) or Jetson host for vision and AI, paired with an ESP32 sub-controller that handles real-time motor control, a 9-axis IMU, and a 2-DOF pan-tilt module carrying a 160° wide-angle camera. Optional LiDAR and depth-camera add-ons plus a ROS 2 build make it an affordable, hackable platform for SLAM, autonomous navigation, and edge-AI experiments.

On Cyberwave, the UGV Beast connects through its digital twin: add it from the catalog, pair the hardware, and drive it from the dashboard, the [Python SDK](/tools/python-sdk), or an AI controller. It streams video and telemetry in real time and supports **autonomous waypoint missions** out of the box — pair it with a cloud VLM to build perception-driven inspection routes. See the [robotic dogs overview](/get-started/robotic-dogs) for the shared mobile-robot workflow.
